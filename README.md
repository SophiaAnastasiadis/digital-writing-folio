# digital-writing-folio
This repository is my folio of work for Digital Writing, in Semester 1 of 2026.

## What types of writing do you do (e.g. fiction, nonfiction, poetry)?
I write primarily fiction, poetry and songs! I have been working on (a series of) novels for about 3 and a half years, although I haven't looked at publishing anything yet.
## What tools (or techniques) do you use to write? Why do you use them?
My favourite application to write on is Google Docs. I used to write with Word but it was the opposite of user-friendly so I switched over to a more streamlined platform. For songwriting, I format my songs on Garageband (because I am cheap).
## How do these tools shape the way you write (e.g., constrain or generate)?
With Google Docs, things are very easy and I'm free to write whatever and however I want. With songwriting it's a little more difficult because I'm confined to whatever loops/instruments/presets are available to me.
## What are curious about writing in the digital environment?
I would love to learn more about creating something that interacts with my readership while they are in the process of reading/consuming it, rather than being a passive observer in a predetermined story.
